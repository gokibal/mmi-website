‘As Pitch perfect in life as in Music’, by Chitravina N. Ravikiran

The Hindu, 26th December 2011.
In this Article, Chitravina N.Ravikiran beautifully explains the sruthi shuddham that was the hallmark of Madurai Mani Iyer’s music. He compares Mani Iyer to Don Bradman by describing his music as Bradmanseque, and sheds light on his impeccable qualities as a human being….

Ms. Lakshmi Devnath says that his music, apart from capturing the public’s imagination was also appreciated by his peers and connoisseurs. She further says that Mani Iyer was a humble man and gives us a glimpse of his other interests……

‘With Madurai Mani Iyer on a rainy evening’, by Charukesi

The Hindu, 25th November 2010.
This was a report on the event conducted to mark Mani Iyer’s 98th Birth anniversary. Rasikas Gathered in large numbers in spite of rain to attend the event and share their memories on the maestro. It also contains an interesting story on how the song ‘Kandan Puriyum Vadivel’, Popularized by Mani Iyer came into existence……
